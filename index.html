Milo the cat

  • June, 2008 - Born with 4 siblings.
  • Nov. 2008 - Was weaned from his mama and was adopted.
  • 2009 - Cutest kitten ever and very playful.
  • 2010 - Milo discovered his love for cuddling.
  • 2011 - Caught his first mouse. I was shocked when he brought it home to me.
  • 2013 - Fond of straying, he found a new home with our neighbour.
  • 2015 - Our neighbour got a new cat from the pound. It was a female cat and Milo found love :)
  • 2016 - I got married and introduced Milo to my wife and of course I was abandoned for my wife. He refused to acknowledge me anymore.
  • 2017 - We had our first kid, Sam. Milo was an adorable cat around the baby.
  • 2020 - Our second baby came and she had the same colour of eyes as Milo. It was a beautiful thing to see especially when Milo was playing the protector.
  • 2021 - The vet told us that Milo had cancer and he didn't have too long to live
  • Feb. 2022 - Milo died and went to the end of the rainbow. He must be the most adorable cat over there. He is forever in our hearts and will be sorely missed.

"The bestest cat I will ever have. I will miss Milo all my life and I will miss playing with Milo. Mommy where is Milo?"

-- Sam at 4 years
